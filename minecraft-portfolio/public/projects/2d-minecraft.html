Features

Procedural World

Fully randomly generated terrain with custom trees, ores, and infinite world generation in all directions.

Block System

Place and break blocks with custom textures. Dig down to find ores or build structures.

Inventory & Crafting

Full inventory system with crafting mechanics to create new items and blocks.

Save & Load

Worlds can be saved and loaded so your progress is never lost.

Entities

A pig entity that roams the world randomly, bringing the world to life.

Custom Textures

Hand-crafted pixel art textures for all blocks and entities.
